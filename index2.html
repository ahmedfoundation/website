Foundation established 2012

The organization was founded in 2012 with the goal of globally assisting in humanitarian efforts providing sustinence to developing nations as well as local community organizations. 

2014 Food Distribution Drive

The program handed out more than 300 lbs of grain to poor local Pakistani residents many whom were too old to make a sustainable living for themselves. 

2015 Dinner for underprivileged

This 2015 Eid event provided a welcoming dinner for people visiting Oklahoma's inner city mosque and community center. 

2017 Food Bag Drive

Hundreds of bags were once again distributed this year to local men and women struggling to afford food on a regular basis.
